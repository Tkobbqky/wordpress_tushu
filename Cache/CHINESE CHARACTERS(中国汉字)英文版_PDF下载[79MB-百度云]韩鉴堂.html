Han Jiantang, a native of Tianjin in north China, is a Chinese languageprofessor with the Tianjin Normal University. He has been teachingChinese to foreign students for 22 years. In 1994, he was sent byMinistry of Education of PRC to teach Chinese in Inner Mongolia'sUlan Bator National University for three years.Works: Chinese Culture （1994）, which was used by the ChinaNational Office for Teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language （Hanbanfor short） and later adopted as a teaching material for MahdarinChinese teaching. Otherworks include Approaching Traditional Chinese Art （2001） producedfor Hanban, and Picture Book on Chinese Characters Culture （2005）which is also used for teaching Chinese as a foreign language. Hehas published a dozen research papers, some of which have wonawards.
